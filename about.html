Our Mission

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate developers and innovators dedicated to simplifying everyday travel. This Fuel Station Locator was born out of a desire to create a fast, reliable, and easy-to-use tool for drivers across India. We believe that technology should solve real-world problems, and finding a fuel stop should be the last of your worries on a long journey.

Our Vision

Our goal is to become the most trusted and comprehensive fuel information platform in the country. We are constantly working to expand our database, add new features like real-time fuel pricing, EV charging station locations, and route planning integration. This project is more than just a locator; it's a step towards smarter, more efficient travel for everyone.

The Team

Built as part of an internship initiative, this project showcases the talent and dedication of emerging tech professionals. We are proud to contribute to public-facing technology and gain invaluable experience in creating user-centric applications.
